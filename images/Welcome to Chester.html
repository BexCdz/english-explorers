Why Chester?

Chester, the Roman heart of the UK

Chester is a city where 2,000 years of history meet vibrant modern life. Founded as a Roman fortress in AD 79, it still showcases its past through ancient city walls, the amphitheatre and the Roman Gardens. The city is equally famous for its medieval charm. The unique Chester Rows, the striking Eastgate Clock and the magnificent cathedral all create a setting full of character and atmosphere. Alongside its heritage, Chester offers riverside walks, independent shops, buzzing restaurants, a world-class zoo and the historic racecourse. It is a place where history, culture and leisure come together, making Chester one of the UK’s most captivating destinations.

My favourite cafes in Chester

Bean & Cole

Bean & Cole is a laid-back coffee shop in Chester known for its expertly brewed specialty coffee and friendly vibe. They serve up tasty brunch dishes, cakes and pastries, making it a favourite spot to relax in the city.

Address

41 Frodsham St, Chester CH1 3JJ, United Kingdom

What I like about it

I love Bean & Cole for its neighbourhood café feel in the heart of Chester. The coffee is always spot on, with single origin beans and rotating guest roasters, and the brunch menu and Kookaburra Bakehouse pastries make it a perfect spot to relax and enjoy. The friendly team and welcoming atmosphere make every visit feel special.

The Flower Cup

A unique, independent botanical coffee shop within the walls of Chester. Stop by for great coffee, all day brunch, great tunes and friendly service.

Address

61 Watergate Street Watergate Row South, Chester CH1 2LE

What I like about it

This café is a great choice for breakfast, brunch or lunch, offering a mix of classic British favourites and healthy options. With vegetarian, vegan and gluten free dishes available, it caters for a wide range of diets. Guests can enjoy free WiFi, convenient digital payments and a dog-friendly atmosphere, making it an ideal spot for both locals and visitors.

Fork and Flavour

Fork & Flavour is a cozy, family-run café by the canal in Chester that’s become a local favourite thanks to its creative brunch menu, excellent coffee and welcoming atmosphere.

Address

61 Watergate Street Watergate Row South, Chester CH1 2LE England

What I like about it

I like Fork & Flavour because it strikes just the right balance between top-quality food and a relaxed setting. The breakfasts and brunches are always full of flavour, made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients, and they do a great job of catering for different diets without feeling like it’s all compromises.
