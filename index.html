Our Story

From the UK to the Heart of Albinyana

Originally from the UK, I moved to this beautiful village five years ago. Having taught English in traditional schools, online, and at home, I realized something vital: learning is about doing.

Surrounded by the stunning landscape of Albinyana, I created English Explorers as a space where students can step away from the desk. We focus on the practical application of English through activities, games, and nature—allowing students to take what they learn at home or school and put those skills into practice in the real world.
